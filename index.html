Stovetop Support

Need help with Stovetop? This is the official support page for the app. Use the contact information below for bug reports, account questions, recipe issues, or general feedback.

Email Support View Privacy Policy

Contact Support

Email: support@stovetopapp.com

When you contact support, include your device model, iOS version, app version, and a short description of the issue so we can help more quickly.

We can help with

  • App crashes or loading problems
  • Recipe saving or organization issues
  • Account and sign-in questions
  • Feature requests and product feedback
  • General troubleshooting

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support with a description of what happened, the device you used, and screenshots if available.

How do I request a feature?

Email your idea and explain how you would use it in Stovetop. We review feature requests as we plan future updates.

What if a recipe does not save correctly?

Make sure you are using the latest version of the app. If the issue continues, contact support with the recipe details and what happened.

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to support requests within 1–3 business days.
