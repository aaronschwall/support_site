Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 22, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Stovetop (“we,” “us,” or “our”) collects, uses, and protects information when you use the Stovetop mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

Depending on how the app is configured, we may collect the following categories of information:

  • Account information, such as your name, email address, or sign-in credentials.
  • User content, including recipes, notes, shopping lists, photos, and other content you choose to save.
  • Device and usage information, such as app version, device type, operating system, crash logs, and diagnostic data.
  • Support communications if you contact us directly.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the app.
  • Sync and store your content across devices, if applicable.
  • Respond to support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • Monitor app performance, reliability, and security.
  • Comply with legal obligations.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information only in these limited situations:

  • With service providers that help us operate the app, such as hosting, analytics, authentication, or support vendors.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect rights, safety, and security.
  • As part of a merger, acquisition, or asset transfer, if applicable.

4. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to provide the app, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements.

5. Your Choices

  • You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of your information, subject to applicable law.
  • You may stop using the app at any time.
  • You may uninstall the app to stop future collection from your device, except where data has already been stored on our systems.

6. Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Stovetop is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can review and delete it if appropriate.

8. International Users

If you use the app from outside your home country, your information may be processed in countries where privacy laws may differ from those in your jurisdiction.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will update the effective date above and may provide additional notice within the app or by other appropriate means.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, contact:

Stovetop Support
support@stovetopapp.com
