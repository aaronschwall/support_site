Help for Stovetop

Welcome to the official support page for Stovetop. If you have questions, run into a bug, need help with recipes or account features, or want to report an issue, this page is the best place to get support.

Contact Support View FAQs

Contact

For help with the app, email us at support@stovetopapp.com.

When possible, include your device type, iOS version, and a short description of the issue so we can help faster.

What we can help with

  • Recipe saving and organization issues
  • App crashes or loading problems
  • Account and sign-in questions
  • Feature requests and feedback
  • Bug reports and usability issues

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I report a bug?

Send an email to support with a short description of what happened, what device you were using, and any screenshots that may help explain the issue.

How do I suggest a feature?

We welcome feedback. Email us with your idea and how you would use the feature in Stovetop.

What if a recipe is not saving correctly?

First, make sure you are on the latest version of the app. If the issue continues, contact support with the recipe details and what happened when you tried to save it.

Do you offer support for account issues?

Yes. For account-related help, contact support and include the email address associated with your account.

Response time

We aim to respond to support requests as quickly as possible. Most inquiries receive a response within 1–3 business days.
